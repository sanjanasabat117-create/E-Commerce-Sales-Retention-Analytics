# E-Commerce Analytics - Dashboard Visualizations 📊

This document provides a detailed walkthrough of the 3-page interactive Power BI dashboard created for this project.

---

## 1. Executive Summary
![Executive Summary](screenshots/dashboard_executive.png)

**Purpose**: Provides a high-level overview of business health for stakeholders.
- **Key KPI**: Achieved a total revenue of **$2.10M** with a healthy **40.82% profit margin**.
- **Global Footprint**: Sales are strongest in the US, but emerging stability is seen in Canada and the UK.
- **Category Performance**: Electronics dominates with over 50% of the revenue share.

---

## 2. Customer Behavior & Funnel
![Customer Behavior](screenshots/dashboard_behavior.png)

**Purpose**: Analyzes how users move through the site and identifies where drop-offs occur.
- **Funnel Efficiency**: We observed a **25K Session to 4K Order conversion** (14.4% funnel efficiency).
- **Device Insights**: Mobile devices drive the highest volume, but Desktop users show a slightly higher conversion consistency.
- **Engagement**: Scatter plots reveal that session duration roughly correlates with higher cart values, emphasizing the need for engaging UI.

---

## 3. Operations & Returns
![Operations and Returns](screenshots/dashboard_operations.png)

**Purpose**: Identifies qualitative issues in products and analyzes revenue leakage due to returns.
- **Return Trends**: Clothing has the highest return rate at 9.2%, primarily due to "Wrong Size" and "Did Not Like".
- **Revenue Leakage**: Identifying the $442k in returns allows for targeted quality control in the Electronics and Clothing categories.
